Welcome to LyricFlow, your all-in-one lyrics application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our application.

  • Device Information
  • Android Version
  • App Version
  • Crash Logs
  • Performance Data
  • Usage Statistics
  • Internet Connection Status

We only collect personal information such as your name only where needed.

3. Lyrics Content

LyricFlow provides song lyrics for educational and personal entertainment purposes. Lyrics remain the property of their respective copyright owners.

4. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Provide better search results
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain app security

5. Permissions

The app may request permissions such as:

  • Internet Access (to fetch lyrics)
  • Network State
  • Storage (to improve search speeds)

Permissions are only used to provide app functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

LyricFlow may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase
  • lrc.lib

These services may collect anonymous information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable methods to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

LyricFlow is not directed toward children under 7 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover such information has been collected, it will be deleted promptly.

9. Cookies

LyricFlow itself does not use cookies. However, third-party services integrated into the app may use cookies or similar technologies.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
